Michael Lombardo

Head of Global Equities

Tradeview Markets

In 1996, Michael graduated Magna Cum Laude from Hofstra University and began his equity career in New York City with Broadway Trading as a proprietary trader. Mr. Lombardo successfully navigated the US equity markets and was named Head Trader where he became responsible for managing a desk of over twenty traders. In 2006, Mr. Lombardo joined Assent LLC in Hoboken, New Jersey, and again successfully navigated the turmoil in the equity markets stemming from the US housing bubble. In 2010, he joined T3 Trading in New York and while continuing his trading career he created the Platinum Development Program, a regimented course focused on teaching new traders about the US equity markets. Mr. Lombardo joined Tradeview Markets in 2017 and oversees all facets of Tradeview's Equity Division
