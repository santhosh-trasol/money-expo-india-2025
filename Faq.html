General Information

Money Expo India 2025 is the country’s premier financial event, bringing together traders, investors, brokers, fintech companies, and industry leaders to explore emerging trends, opportunities, and innovations in financial markets.

Money Expo India 2025 will be held on August 23-24, 2025, at the Jio World Convention Centre, Mumbai, India.

The event is open to investors, traders, financial professionals, fintech innovators, brokers, and anyone interested in financial markets.

The event will run from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM on both days. View Speakers
